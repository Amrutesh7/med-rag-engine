Privacy Policy

Effective: March 2026 Last reviewed: March 2026

Data Collection

Section 01

This application does not collect or store any personal user data. We do not maintain user accounts, personal profiles, or any persistent records tied to an individual.

No registration, authentication, or sign-in is required to use this service.

Query Processing

Section 02

Queries entered into the system may be processed temporarily to generate responses using a retrieval-augmented generation (RAG) system. This processing is ephemeral — queries are not persisted to any database, log, or storage medium beyond the scope of a single request-response cycle.

Query content is not shared with third parties, sold, or used for any purpose other than generating the immediate response presented to you.

Tracking & Analytics

Section 03

No cookies, tracking pixels, session identifiers, fingerprinting techniques, or third-party analytics platforms (e.g., Google Analytics) are currently implemented.

There are no advertisement networks, remarketing scripts, or behavioural tracking systems embedded in this application.

Intended Use

Section 04

This system is intended exclusively for demonstration, educational, and research purposes. It is not a commercial product and should not be used as a basis for clinical decision-making or patient care.

Your privacy is protected by design. This system processes queries in-memory only. No personal data is written to disk, transmitted to third parties, or retained after your session ends. Questions about this policy? Contact support.
